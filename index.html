About Me

Ali A. Noroozi

Ph.D. student of computer science
University of Tabriz
email: noroozi AT tabrizu DOT ac DOT ir

I am a Ph.D. student of computer science at University of Tabriz, under supervision of Dr. Jaber Karimpour and Dr. Ayaz Isazadeh. My research interests are security, quantification of information leakage, secure information flow, malware analysis, and model checking.

I received a B.Sc. degree in Information Technology Engineering from Amirkabir University of Tehran in 2009, an M.S. Eng. degree in Management of Information Systems from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran in 2011.
