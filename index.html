About Me

Ali A. Noroozi

PhD, Computer Science
Freelancer, Python and machine learning developer
email: noroozi AT tabrizu DOT ac DOT ir

I work as a freelancer. I develop machine learning applications in Python. I enjoy working with Python SciKit-Learn, NLTK, NetworkX, Numpy, Scipy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. Recently, I've been working on deep learning models, inluding CNNs, RNNs, and Adversarial Neural Networks. I develop NLP deep models using Tensorflow and Keras.

I got my Ph.D. at University of Tabriz, under supervision of Dr. Jaber Karimpour and Dr. Ayaz Isazadeh. My research interests are security, quantification of information leakage, secure information flow, malware analysis, and model checking.

I received a B.Sc. degree in Information Technology Engineering from Amirkabir University of Tehran in 2009, an M.S. Eng. degree in Management of Information Systems from K. N. Toosi University of Technology, Tehran in 2011.
